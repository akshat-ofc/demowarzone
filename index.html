Regulations

Know The Rules

01
Squad of Four

Every competing unit must consist of exactly four players. Solo registrations, trios, and duos will not be accepted under any circumstances.

02
Valid BGMI UID Required

All four players must submit their correct 10-digit BGMI UID. UIDs are verified before match room access is granted. Invalid UIDs = disqualification.

03
No Emulators

Participation is restricted to mobile devices only. Playing on emulators is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate disqualification of the entire squad.

04
Zero Tolerance Policy

Hacks, aimbots, macros, wall-glitches, or any third-party software that provides unfair advantage are banned outright. Reports are reviewed and acted upon swiftly.

05
Match Punctuality

All four players must join the room within five minutes of the room ID being shared. A grace period of 5 minutes is allowed; late arrivals will not be accommodated.

06
Entry Fee

A non-refundable entry fee of ₹200 per squad is required to confirm the registration slot. Payment details will be provided after form submission.

Support

Questions Answered

Can the same player be in two different squads? +
No. Each BGMI UID can only be registered once. Duplicate UID entries across different squads will result in both squads being disqualified without a refund.
What happens if one of our players goes absent on match day? +
Player substitutions are not allowed once registration is closed. If a player is absent, the squad must play with remaining members or forfeit their match slot. No refunds will be issued for absent players.
Is there an age restriction or minimum rank to enter? +
All players must be at least 16 years of age and residents of India. A minimum in-game rank of Platinum is required at time of registration. Crown, Ace, and Conqueror players are encouraged to participate.
How and when are prizes distributed? +
Prize money is transferred to the squad captain's UPI within 7 working days after the tournament concludes. TDS deductions apply for amounts exceeding ₹10,000 as per Indian tax regulations.
Can we make changes to our squad after registration? +
Squad details can only be updated up to 72 hours before the first match day. After that window closes, all submitted details are considered final and no changes will be accepted.
How will we receive the match room IDs? +
Room IDs are sent exclusively to the squad captain's registered phone number via WhatsApp, 30 minutes before each scheduled match. Make sure the phone number is active and reachable on match days.

Terms & Conditions

BGMI Warzone Cup 2025 · Last updated: July 1, 2025

01
Eligibility Criteria
  • All participants must be a minimum of 16 years of age at the time of registration.
  • Participation is open to residents of India only. International players are not eligible.
  • Each player must hold a minimum BGMI in-game rank of Platinum at the time of squad registration.
  • Players currently serving a ban on any BGMI platform or previous esports event are ineligible.
  • Organizers reserve the right to verify rank and account details before granting tournament access.
02
Team Registration Rules
  • Each squad must consist of exactly four (4) players. Partial squads will not be accepted.
  • No player may be registered under more than one squad. Duplicate UIDs will result in disqualification of all involved squads.
  • All information submitted at registration is considered final once the form is submitted and entry fee is paid.
  • Changes to player details may only be requested up to 72 hours before the first match day, subject to organizer approval.
  • Entry fees are non-refundable once payment is confirmed, except in the case of organizer-initiated cancellation.
03
Fair Play Policy
  • The use of any form of hacking software, aimbots, wall hacks, speed hacks, or any client-side modification is strictly prohibited.
  • Emulator-based gameplay is not permitted. All players must compete on iOS or Android mobile devices.
  • Match-fixing, score manipulation, or any collusion between squads to influence results is a bannable offense.
  • Screenshots and screen recordings may be requested by organizers at any time to validate gameplay integrity.
⚠ Violation Consequence: Any squad found in breach of fair play rules will be immediately disqualified, permanently banned from future events, and their entry fee will be forfeited.
04
Match Conduct & Scheduling
  • Room IDs will be shared to the squad captain's registered phone number via WhatsApp, 30 minutes before each scheduled match.
  • A 5-minute grace period is granted from the scheduled start time. Squads failing to join within this window forfeit their slot for that match.
  • All matches must be played in the scheduled mode and map as communicated by the organizers.
  • Intentional disconnections to avoid unfavorable outcomes will be treated as a forfeiture.
  • Technical issues on a player's end do not constitute grounds for a rematch.
05
Entry Fee & Payments
  • The registration entry fee is ₹200 per squad, payable via UPI to details provided after form submission.
  • Entry fees are strictly non-refundable once payment is confirmed, regardless of the squad's performance in the tournament.
  • In the event of tournament cancellation by the organizers, full refunds will be processed within 10 working days.
  • Squads have 24 hours from form submission to complete payment. Unpaid slots will be released and made available to waitlisted squads.
06
Prize Distribution
  • Prize money will be transferred to the squad captain's UPI ID within 7 working days following the tournament's conclusion.
  • TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) will be applied to prize amounts exceeding ₹10,000 as mandated by Indian tax law.
  • In the event of a tie in final standings, the squad with the higher total kills across all matches will be ranked higher.
  • Prize certificates will be issued digitally to all winning squad members within 14 days of the tournament conclusion.
07
Code of Conduct
  • All players are expected to behave in a sportsmanlike and respectful manner towards opponents, organizers, and spectators at all times.
  • Hate speech, discriminatory language, harassment, or threats of any kind directed at any individual are strictly prohibited.
  • Disputes must be raised through official channels only. Publicly disputing results on social media prior to organizer review may result in penalties.
  • The squad captain is responsible for the conduct of all squad members throughout the tournament duration.
08
Organizer's Rights & Modifications
  • The organizing team reserves the right to amend, suspend, or cancel the tournament at any time without prior notice.
  • All decisions made by the organizing team regarding rules enforcement, disqualifications, and prize distribution are final and binding.
  • Organizers may modify match schedules, formats, or maps at their discretion if required for operational reasons.
  • The organizers reserve the right to disqualify any squad deemed to be acting in a manner contrary to the spirit of the tournament.

  • Squad names, IGNs, and match results may be published publicly on the organizer's social media channels and website.
  • Player data will not be sold or shared with third parties for commercial purposes.
